OUR JOURNEY

CHOCOPUFF WAS ESTABLISHED IN 2026

Our founder started experimenting in the kitchen at the age of 17, constantly chasing the perfect balance between fluffy inside and rich flavors throughout his high school and college years. He successfully perfected the signature marshmallow recipe in late 2024, after which he embarked on a journey to craft this ultimate dessert experience in early 2025. By 2026, he had fulfilled his vision and officially established Chocopuff to share these sweet creations with the world.

Our professional processes resolutely focus on gourmet, hand-crafted dessert pastries within the confectionery arena, blending premium cocoa with glass-like marshmallow fillings. Adding another layer of expertise to the repertoire, we have mastered the art of texture combinations—ensuring every roll achieves the perfect bite. Our role encompasses diverse responsibilities such as sourcing the finest ingredients, managing daily baking production, organizing flavor profiles, devising unique recipes, and ensuring smooth, fresh delivery to our customers.

The Chocopuff Team possesses an extensive grasp of the intricacies involved in the baking and design process, dedicated in creation to effective flavor planning and execution.

OUR MILESTONES

The key moments, late-night breakthroughs, and sweet victories that shaped Chocopuff.

Late 2024

A Sweet Footnote

The journey began as a passion project in a humble kitchen. While studying classic literature late into the night, our founder started experimenting with marshmallow and cocoa combinations—creating a bold, delicious "footnote to youth" that rewrote the rules of snacking.

Mid 2025

The High-Velocity Fuel

To power through intense morning cycling routes, the kitchen team developed a high-crunch, portable prototype. Tested against the elements on fast downhill sprints, the bar evolved to deliver an instantaneous snap and long-lasting energy.

Late 2025

The "With High Honors" Formula

After endless revisions and strict testing, the signature recipe achieved a flawless 95-point internal sensory rating. The breakthrough earned "High Honors" from early taste-testers, solidifying the base texturing of our three primary flavors.

March 27, 2026

The Convocation of Taste

Chocopuff officially stepped into the limelight! Celebrated as our grand convocation day, the brand debuted its polished packaging to the public, setting a new premium standard for timeless flavor execution.
